The Path to Wellness

A seamless, clinically sound process directly inside your space.

01.

Schedule Your Appointment

Select your IV therapy based on your current health needs via our secure platform. Choose a date, time, and location—whether that’s your home, your office, or a hotel room. Immediate dispatches are available depending on nurse proximity.

02.

Nurse Arrival & Consult

A fully licensed Registered Nurse will arrive with pharmaceutical-grade supplies. They will perform a brief health assessment, check your vitals, and ensure the chosen IV drip is optimal for your body.

03.

Hydrate & Recover

Sit back on your favorite couch or bed. The administration takes approximately 30-45 minutes. Because the vitamins are delivered directly into your bloodstream, you will feel the rejuvenating effects almost instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does IV Therapy hurt?

Our nurses are experts in venipuncture. Some patients feel a tiny pinch during insertion, but the therapy itself is painless and most patients find it highly relaxing.

How closely regulated are the vitamins?

All our formulations are sourced from FDA-approved 503B compounding pharmacies and overseen by our Medical Director, a board-certified physician.

Can I customize my IV bag?

Absolutely. Our Registered Nurses offer a pre-infusion consultation where you can elect to add extra vitamins such as Zofran, Toradol, B12, or Glutathione.
